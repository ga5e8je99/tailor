CUSTOM-MADE
Endless Possibilities
Stop by our store to experience all we have to offer: tailoring & alterations, made-to-measure, bespoke, and made-to-order. Design your perfect wardrobe today!
Tailoring & Alteration
we pride ourselves on our high end style of tailoring. We offer alterations for both men and women in formal traditional garments as well as casual wear.
Made-to-Order
This is our small batch production made just for you. We give you a range of custom design options for a specific garment (colors, buttons, style, etc.) and, once ordered, we begin working on it immediately.
Made-to-Measure
Made-to-measure is a custom based on our exclusive fitting block, also known as our try-on. With this try-on, we use 35 distinct measurements to ensure you are designing a garment with a remarkable fit!
LODGE A INQUIRY

Where style meets convenience… Your tailor will meet you at your home, office, and/or on location. Our experience gives you full control of he design process in creating a unique garment tailored exclusively for you.
